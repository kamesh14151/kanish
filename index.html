About Me

I'm a passionate social activist and community leader dedicated to creating positive change in society. My work focuses on empowering marginalized communities, advocating for social justice, and combining technical expertise with artistic expression.

Through grassroots organizing, community engagement, and leveraging my diverse skill set, I strive to address pressing social issues and create sustainable solutions that improve people's lives. My approach combines empathy, strategic thinking, and collaborative action.

I believe in the power of collective action to transform communities and create a more equitable society. Every initiative I undertake is guided by principles of inclusivity, transparency, and sustainable impact.

Art & Technology Empowerment

Currently leading a comprehensive Art & Technology Empowerment Program designed to provide young people with creative and technical skills needed to become agents of change in their communities.

My Role: Program Director - Responsible for program design, implementation, and evaluation. Combining artistic expression with technical education to create holistic development opportunities.

The program includes art workshops, technical training in Python and cybersecurity, community service projects, and leadership development. Our goal is to empower 500+ youth across 10 communities over the next two years.
